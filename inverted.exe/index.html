about us

about

inverted.exe is an emerging t-shirt brand born in the heart of central java, redefining streetwear culture through bold designs and uncompromising creativity. we are architects of the unconventional, crafting wearable art that challenges the status quo and celebrates the raw energy of indonesian design.

our vision is simple yet bold: to create tees that resonate globally while staying rooted in our local heritage. every design is a rebellion against the ordinary—a carefully orchestrated fusion of tradition, innovation, and contemporary culture.

mission

we are committed to elevating indonesian streetwear on the global stage. our mission is to produce limited-edition, groundbreaking designs that tell stories worth wearing and build a community of visionaries who refuse to settle for mediocrity.

we believe in intentional creation. quality fabrics. meaningful graphics. substance over trend. every tee carries purpose, every drop matters. we are here to make 2025 the year indonesia's streetwear scene becomes unstoppable.
